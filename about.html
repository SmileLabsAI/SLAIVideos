About Smile Labs Videos

Smile Labs Videos is a video production company specialized in Dentistry, utilizing artificial intelligence for the creation, research, and editing of visual content. Our goal is to offer innovative and educational videos for dentists and professionals in the field, assisting in communication with their patients and in presenting procedures.

Dentists can acquire our content in two ways: purchasing individual video packages or becoming members of the Smile Labs AI community, gaining full access to our library of exclusive productions.

How Can the Videos Help?

Smile Labs Videos are created to demonstrate dental procedures in a clear and professional manner. They can be used to:

  • Educate patients about aesthetic and oral health treatments.
  • Assist in the decision-making process, providing more clarity and confidence.
  • Display on screens in the office waiting room, sparking interest in new treatments.

Part of the Smile Labs AI Ecosystem

Smile Labs Videos is part of the Smile Labs AI ecosystem, an innovation hub in Dentistry, which in the future will include:

  • Smile Labs 3D – Digital smile scanning.
  • Blockchain & Smart Contracts – NFT marketplace for digitized smiles.
  • SLAI Coin – A cryptocurrency for the community, bringing exclusive benefits.
